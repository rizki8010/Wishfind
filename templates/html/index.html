Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence that teaches computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. By using algorithms and statistical models, computers can identify patterns in data and use those patterns to make predictions or decisions without human intervention. Machine learning is used in various fields, including facial recognition, speech recognition, data analysis, natural language processing, and many more.

Energy Efficiency

Heating load refers to the amount of heat that needs to be added to a room to maintain the indoor air temperature at a comfortable level during the winter season. It is crucial to ensure that the room remains warm and comfortable for its occupants, regardless of the temperatures outside the building.

Cooling load, on the other hand, is the amount of heat that needs to be removed from a room to maintain the indoor air temperature at a comfortable level during the summer season. Rooms can gain heat from various sources such as external loads (such as sunlight), internal loads (such as electronic equipment), air infiltration, and ventilation. It is important to remove excess heat from the room to keep it comfortable for occupancy.
